## Red Flags to Watch For

### Evidence Destruction Indicators
- Missing pages or documents
- Altered dates or amounts
- Deleted emails or files
- Destroyed physical records
- Uncooperative custodians

### Fraud Indicators
- Negative inventory balances
- 10x year-over-year changes
- No write-off recovery
- Below-cost related party sales
- Unallocated expenses for extended periods

### Control Indicators
- Use of others' email addresses
- Unknown trustees or directors
- Concealed ownership structures
- Conflicts of interest (family relationships)
- Self-dealing transactions
