### Legal Violations

- Acting in bad faith and for improper purpose
- Failure to act in best interests of companies

- Unauthorized use of company assets

#### Section 77: Liability of Directors
- Willful misconduct in execution of duties
- Breach of fiduciary trust

- Fraudulent conduct causing financial loss

- Asset stripping and systematic misappropriation
- Failure to maintain proper accounting records
