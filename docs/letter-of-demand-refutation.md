## The Seven Allegations and Refutations

**Jax's Allegation:** Rynette instructed customers to divert payments to unauthorized accounts, depleting company funds.

**Refutation:** **FALSE DENIAL**

The evidence provides irrefutable proof that Rynette orchestrated a systematic payment diversion scheme:

- **Forensic Bank Records** show specific dates, amounts, and destination accounts for diverted payments
- **Bank Statement Screenshots** visually confirm payments from customers deposited into unauthorized accounts
- **Financial Analysis** quantifies the exact losses from the payment diversion scheme
- **Verification Matrix** cross-references specific customer payments to wrong accounts

**Conclusion:** Rynette's denial is a falsehood. Jax's allegation is proven true by the evidence.
