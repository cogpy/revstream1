# Financial Impact Analysis

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-14

This analysis provides a detailed breakdown of the financial impact of the fraudulent scheme in case 2025-137857, which exceeds **R10.27 million**.

---

## Hijacked Revenue Stream

The primary financial impact is the hijacked revenue stream, which totals **R10,269,727.90**. This is the amount of money that was diverted from the legitimate business to the perpetrators' accounts.

**Evidence:**
- **[JF03: Financial Records](../../evidence/financial.md#jf03-financial-records-and-analysis):** Provides the raw data for the financial analysis.
- **[JF07: Sage System Screenshots](../../evidence/critical.md#jf07-sage-system-screenshots-and-payment-records):** Shows the fraudulent transactions and the diversion of funds.

## Platform Investment

In addition to the hijacked revenue, the victims also lost their investment in the Shopify Plus platform, which amounted to **R140,000 - R280,000** over a period of 28 months.

**Evidence:**
- **[JF01: Shopify Plus Email](../../evidence/critical.md#jf01-shopify-plus-email-26-july-2017---the-forensic-time-capsule):** Establishes the timeline of the platform investment.
