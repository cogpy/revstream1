## Companies Act 71 of 2008

### Section 75: Conflict of Interest

**Requirements:**
- Director must **disclose** personal financial interest in matter before company
- Director must **recuse** from decisions affecting the other company
- Failure → nullification of decisions & personal liability

**Peter's Violations:**
- ✅ Serves as director of **both** RST (Company A) and RegimA SA (Company B)
- ✅ Failed to disclose conflicts in revenue allocation decisions
- ✅ Failed to recuse from decisions benefiting RST at expense of RegimA SA
- ✅ Participated in decisions with personal interest

**Evidence:**
- Board minutes (required)
- Financial records showing revenue diversion
- Decision records affecting revenue allocation

**Burden of Proof:** 50% (civil) - **ACHIEVABLE** with board minutes

---

### Section 76: Fiduciary Duties

**Duties Owed to Each Company:**
- Act in **good faith** and for proper purpose
- Act in **best interests** of the company
- **Avoid conflicts** of interest
- Not **misuse position** or information
- Exercise **care, skill and diligence**

**Peter's Violations Toward RegimA SA:**
- ✅ Diverted corporate opportunity from RegimA SA to RST
- ✅ Acted **against** interests of RegimA SA
- ✅ Used position to benefit RST **unlawfully**
- ✅ Instructed Rynette to commit **fraud** against RegimA SA
- ✅ Failed to act in **good faith** toward RegimA SA
- ✅ Exposed RegimA SA to **legal risk**

**Evidence:**
- Financial records showing R10,269,727.90 diversion
- Sage system control by Rynette (Pete@regima.com)
- Bank transfer records
- Fraudulent records for RegimA SA

**Burden of Proof:** 50% (civil) - **EXCEEDED**

### Section 162: Delinquent Director

**Statutory Grounds (Court MUST Declare Delinquent If):**
- Grossly **abused position**
- Intentionally **inflicted harm** on company
- Acted **dishonestly**
- Committed **fraud**
- Acted with **gross negligence**

**Peter Meets ALL Grounds:**
- ✅ Grossly abused position as director of RegimA SA
- ✅ Intentionally inflicted harm (R10,269,727.90)
- ✅ Acted dishonestly by instructing fraud
- ✅ Committed fraud against RegimA SA
- ✅ Acted with gross negligence

**Consequences:**

**Potential Applicants:**

**Burden of Proof:** 50% (civil) - **EXCEEDED**
