# PLATFORM_002 - Courier Guy

**Entity Type:** Platform  
**Type:** Logistics Platform  
**Role:** Delivery Service

---

## Overview

Courier Guy is a third-party logistics provider used by RegimA Worldwide Distribution for shipment and delivery of customer orders. The platform was identified in the Rynette Organogram (2025-04-12) as an integral part of the sales fulfillment process.

---

## Services Provided

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Shipment Loading Portal | Orders loaded onto Courier Guy system |
| Tracking Number Generation | Unique tracking numbers for each shipment |
| Parcel Collection | Physical collection of parcels from warehouse |
| Delivery Notification | Customer notification of delivery status |
| Return Handling | Management of unsuccessful deliveries and returns |

---

## Role in Sales Process

According to the Rynette Organogram, Courier Guy performs the following functions in the sales fulfillment chain:

1. **Shipment Loading**: Once an order is marked fulfilled in Shopify, the shipment is loaded on the Courier Guy portal
2. **Tracking Generation**: A tracking number is generated for customer visibility
3. **Collection**: Courier Guy collects parcels from the RegimA warehouse
4. **Delivery**: Parcels are delivered to customers, with notification of any problems
5. **Status Reporting**: Final notification of delivery success or parcel return

## Evidence Significance

Courier Guy provides **third-party verification** of order fulfillment, creating an independent audit trail that can corroborate or contradict internal records. This is significant for:

| Application | Relevance |
